Biography

I’m Sebastián Ferrada, a Doctoral student at the Center for Semantic Web Research at Universidad de Chile. My research interests include Information Retrieval, Data Mining & Visualization, Multimedia Databases and Linked Data. I’m currently developing my PhD thesis regarding the combination of semantic and visual similarity queries using Semantic Web technologies such as RDF and SPARQL, you can learn more by visiting the IMGpedia project site.

Besides research I’m very into teaching. Currently I’m Teaching Assistant in Databases and Programming Methodologies. I have worked in many other areas and courses, you may find a complete list having a look at my resumé. I have done volunteer teaching at Preumed, Capsocial and Apoyo Escolar.

Selected Publications

IMGpedia: Enriching the Web of Data with Image Content Analisys

Linked Data rarely takes into account multimedia content, which forms a central part of the Web. To explore the combination of Linked Data and multimedia, we are developing IMGpedia: we compute content-based descriptors for images used in Wikipedia articles and subsequently propose to link these descriptions with legacy encyclopaedic knowledge-bases such as DBpedia and Wikidata.
Sebastián Ferrada, Benjamin Bustos, Aidan Hogan
In Alberto Mendelzon Workshop on Foundations of Data Management.
